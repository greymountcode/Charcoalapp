About Us

Our mission is to produce high-quality, clean charcoal that minimizes environmental impact through sustainable sourcing, cutting-edge technology, and responsible production practices. We are committed to not only offering customers a cleaner, greener alternative, but also enhancing social impact by creating job opportunities for elderly women in suburban and rural communities. Through these efforts, we aim to contribute to global carbon reduction, waste minimization, and a healthier, more inclusive world for future generations.

Our Products

Coconut Charcoal Briquette

Coconut charcoal briquettes are rapidly gaining popularity not only for grilling and cooking but also as a superior choice for shisha (hookah) smoking. Their clean burn, long-lasting heat, and eco-friendly properties make them an ideal alternative to traditional charcoal.

Halaban Wood Charcoal

Halaban Wood Charcoal stands out as a superior alternative to traditional wood charcoal due to its cleaner, more efficient, and eco-friendly burning properties.
